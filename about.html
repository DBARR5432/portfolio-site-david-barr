My Story

Over the last several years I have decided a change in profession would be beneficial. It was a difficult decision to leave music, but I believe my experience in that field will actually be a benefit as I move forward in a different field that still has many creative elements within it.

I’d tried a few coding courses in the past, but nothing that really clicked with me. In the Spring of 2022 I took a Project Management course through Google, as I thought my soft skills would translate well. While I think I could’ve continued learning and possibly become a PM at some point, all that course really did was make me realize I don’t really want to be a project manager.

A while later I started looking into UX/UI design, and decided that UI was a field that interested me. Now, a little more than a year later I have completed the Career Foundry UI Design course, and I’m simply trying to further develop my skills, refine my portfolio, and see if I can turn all this education into a new career path!
